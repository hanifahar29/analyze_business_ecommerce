# Project Scope: Analyzing eCommerce Business Performance with SQL

## Background / Purpose

This project analyzes the business performance of one of the largest eCommerce marketplaces in South America that connects micro-business owners with their customers.

**Goals:**
- Analyze customer growth trends over time
- Evaluate product quality based on revenue and cancellation rate
- Understand payment type usage patterns and trends

The findings from this analysis are expected to provide data-driven insights to support business decision making.

---

## Business Questions

### 1. Customer Growth
- How is the trend of new customer acquisition over time?
- How is the trend of active customers per year?
- How many customers made repeat orders?

### 2. Product Quality
- What product categories generated the most revenue?
- What product categories had the highest number of cancelled orders?
- How is the trend of revenue per product category per year?

### 3. Payment Type
- What payment methods are most frequently used by customers?
- How is the trend of each payment type usage over time?

---

## Scope

| Area | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Customer Growth | Analysis of new and active customers based on order history |
| Product Quality | Analysis of revenue and cancellation rate per product category |
| Payment Type | Analysis of payment method usage trends |

---

## Out of Scope

- This project does not include analysis outside the three defined aspects (customer growth, product quality, payment type)
- No real-time data analysis
- No predictive or machine learning modeling
- Implementation of business recommendations is not included
